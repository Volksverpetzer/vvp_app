The official Volksverpetzer app brings fact checks, analysis, and articles directly to your mobile phone.

Use the app to read, search, save, and share Volksverpetzer content in a mobile-friendly interface.

Features:
- Read articles and the social media feeds from Volksverpetzer
- Search for topics
- Save favorites and article sources
- Share content
- Push notifications for new content (depending on device settings)

The app is open source under the MIT license and the source code is published on GitHub.
